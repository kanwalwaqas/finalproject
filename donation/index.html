Thank You

Thank you so much for deciding to support Islamic Relief with a monthly Direct Debit. Dedicated to alleviating the suffering of the world's poorest people, we couldn't do it without generous donations from people like you. The Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him, said that "The most beloved deed to God is the most regular and constant" - your regular gifts will help us to plan ahead, ensuring that we offer the best long-term help possible, and are always ready to answer the call.
